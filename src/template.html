    About me

    I am a UX/UI designer with an aptitude for solving problems and a desire to learn. The opportunities I have had in my career to improve experiences and make people's lives easier have helped me realize that I want to spend my time creating customer-centered products.

    My background in operations and marketing and my web development skills allow me to uniquely bridge the gap between design, development, and business teams. I bring an understanding of business needs, user priorities, and the technical practicality of designs.

    I am also passionate about health, natural living, food, and wildlife rehabilitation.
